Bridging Industry & Academia

The rapid pace of technological innovation with the advent of Artificial Intelligence (AI) means that a traditional engineering curriculum, while foundational, needs to be supplemented with practical, hands-on training on the latest tools and technologies. With this end in view, we plan to work in partnership with colleges, where the colleges take care of the academic performance of the students, while we address their skilling and practical training needs.

In this regard, we have partnered with Mysuru Royal Institute of Technology (MRIT), an engineering college in Mandya, Karnataka.MRIT was established in the year 2015. The founders of the college are all eminent retired professors of engineering colleges.

Our partnership with MRIT works on both the tracks - of (a) academic learning, and (b) skilling / practical training - progress in parallel for 12 months - right through the 6th and 7th semester of the engineering studies.

Early outcomes of this collaboration are very promising, with several MRIT students of the 2023-24 and 2024-25 passing out batch finding placements in companies like Deloitte, Zeta, Ignite IQ, Thought Process, Socgen and Rythmos, with competitive salary packages.

Our Comprehensive Training Approach

A holistic learning experience designed to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements

🎯 Core Training Components

Structured Internship

The students are then put through a 500+ hours of an intensive and structured internship program that helps students to understand the real-world industry expectations in software projects.

Aptitude Training

Students are made to solve logical reasoning, solve mathematical problems, and verbal skills so that they confidently crack the aptitude rounds during the interview process.

Communication Skills

In order to enhance teamwork and collaboration, good communication becomes the foundation of a successful team. We train students on Spoken English through English Helper's proven 'English Bolo' program.

🚀 Advanced Support Services

Performance Tracking & Feedback

We provide continuous personalized feedback - both to the students and MRIT - that drives consistent improvement, by tracking the students performance in real time.

Mock Interviews

To prepare the students for the hiring process, we conduct mock interviews with the help of industry experts. These interactions help students to get prepared for the interviews, group discussions, and portfolio building exercises.

Individual Mentoring

In order to rebuild confidence and address personal challenges in students, we work on tailored one-on-one mentoring, wherever required.

💼 Project & Career Support

Mini Projects & Final Year Project

We offer support to students by giving them mini projects focusing on practical aspects that the industry expects, and also assist them for their final year project completion.

Resume & Interview Preparation

Our team led by industry professionals support students in smart resume crafting and comprehensive interview preparation strategies.

Placement Assistance

Our ultimate goal is to see these up-skilled students succeed. We leverage our network of IT companies to facilitate placements, connecting students with companies for internships and full-time roles.

Hear from the Principal

Information Technology today is changing faster than ever, with AI changing every previous rule. The IT industry today cannot afford long lead times to make their freshers productive, and is therefore looking for job-ready talent.

As a sincere academic institution, committed to give our students the best chance of a successful career, we found it a huge challenge to meet this industry demand successfully. To overcome this challenge, we have now partnered with Exskilence Upskilling Bengaluru, a firm that is focused on skilling and placement support. The results of this association have been very heartening so far.

The Exskilence team is extremely committed to making the student truly job-ready. Their program delivers intense practical training using their own proprietary Learning Management System and Coding Platform. This is supplemented further by detailed on-site practical workshops. Personally, what I liked most about their program is how they track each student’s progress every day. And this is shared with both the student and the college, for suitable action.

I am extremely optimistic of achieving some great results for our students through this partnership with Exskilence in the years to come.

Dr. Abhinandan

Principal,
